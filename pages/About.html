About Us


Experts in the ICT industry since 1999. We create industry specific solutions that are easily accessible for small to medium organisations and blue-chip companies.


Breathing new life into business

At Inspirit we have years of experience within the ERP landscape driving integration, automation and optimization. We have a blend of OEM and custom developed solutions to enhance your business outputs to ultimately make your organisation more efficient and effective. Over the last decade we have harnessed the power of data driven solutions to provide our customers with analytical and tailored data solutions to meet their business needs.

We are a South African based organisation with an EMEA (Europe, the Middle East and Africa) footprint and have partnered with some of Africa's largest ICT companies like SYSPRO, IOCO, MIE, and XDS giving us access to a broad range of solutions. data products and access to specialised resources to optimise your business. At the Inspirit group of companies, we create industry specific solutions that are easily accessible for small to medium organisations and blue-chip companies

What we do

Inspirit provides insights to companies and assists in unlocking their unknown potential or hidden opportunities by delivering top quality, business intelligence and analytic solutions under one umbrella.

The Inspirit group of companies prides ourselves on being change management agents and are passionate about " breathing new life into your business".
