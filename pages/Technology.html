Key technologies in Fincyber's software


Ensure compliance and reduce risk with our
advanced business verification solution.


Customer Onbarding

It emcompasses a series of steps aimed at familiarizing customers with what the business offers, establishing a positive initial experience, and facilitating their transition into becoming active and engaged users.

Address Verification Service

A security feature commonly used in payment processing to help verify the identity of cardholder during a transaction. AVS compares the billing address provided by the card holder with the address on file with the card issuer.

Anti-money Laundering

Anit-money Laundering refers to a set of laws, regulations, and procedures designed to prevent criminals from disguising illegally obtained funds as legitimate income.

Optical Character Recognition

Fincyber's Optical Character Recognition (OCR) engine, converts images into machine readable text. It is used to extract important information from the identity, proof of address and bank account documentation and then validates the extracted information to 3rd party database sources.

Fincyber Validation

Fincyber's FICA process offers a customizable workflow that includes the following checks: ID Verification (ID number, name and surname), Address, KYC, Bank Account Verification, Facial Recognition, World Checks - AML, PIPS and PEPs

Liveness Detection

A security feature commonly used in payment processing to help verify the identity of a cardholder during a transaction. AVS compares the billing address provided by the card holder with the address on file with the card issuer.
